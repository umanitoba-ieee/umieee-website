Our Mission

University of Manitoba Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers Student Branch (UMIEEE) is a non-profit organization managed by a team of dedicated student volunteers. Our mission is to provide students with the space and equipment necessary to maximize learning opportunities and enhance education. UMIEEE provides a laboratory environment congruent to the Electrical and Computer Engineering labs operated by the University of Manitoba, enabling students to expand their knowledge outside of the classroom.

UMIEEE strives to engage students through workshops and networking events, enriching knowledge and developing professional communication skills. Each academic year we host technical workshops covering Arduino, Raspberry Pi, embedded devices, and LaTeX typesetting. Our networking events connect students directly with professional practicing engineers.

IEEE Advantages

Members of the University of Manitoba IEEE branch are offered several exclusive benefits and spaces.

Lounge Area

A dedicated space for studying, relaxing, and hanging out.

  • Canteen (prices posted inside)
  • Classroom whiteboards and markers
  • Massage Chair for stress relief
  • External monitors for productivity
  • Large conference table for meetings

Lab Area

A fully-equipped electronics laboratory for your projects.

  • Soldering equipment and stations
  • Arduino boards & Raspberry Pi's
  • High-performance computer access

Extra Perks

Additional benefits of being a UMIEEE member.

  • Free-of-charge secure lockers
  • Partnerships with UMRT, UMSATS, UMSAE,
  • Discounted or free workshop entry

Join Us

Follow these steps to become a UMIEEE member.

  1. Navigate to the Join IEEE webpage.
  2. Select the "JOIN AS A STUDENT” option.
  3. Create your IEEE account and complete your membership order.
  4. Complete our UMIEEE Membership Form (Have your IEEE number ready).
  5. Join the UMIEEE Discord Server.
  6. Follow our UMIEEE Instagram.
